The visualisation is trying to put together major historical events, describing the loss of 200 years of India.

Why British owe India a reparation?

Debate at Oxford UNION by Dr. Shashi Tharoor

May 2015

England
The rule of 200 years gave a financial rise to Britain by depredation of India through all means of subjugating and seizing power, trading in silk, spices other profitable Indian commodities.
At Oxford Union on ‘Does Britain owes India a reparation’, Shashi Tharoor as a speaker spoke in favour of the topic. This winning debate got viral as soon as he twitted the link. The important insight he came up with was that even most of the educated people were also not aware of the loot which happened during the time of British Raj.
